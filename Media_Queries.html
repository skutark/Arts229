Who is Temples?

Temples are English rock band from England formed in 2012 by singer and guitarist James Bagshaw and bassist Tom Warmsley. The rest of the band is band up of the other two members, the keyboardist and rhythm guitarist Adam Smith, and the dummer Samuel Toms.

The band has released two studio albums, two EP's, and a number of singles. Their debut album, Sun Structures, was released in 2014 and charted at number seven in the UK. They are signed to Heavenly Records and have toured internationally both as a support act and as the headliner.
